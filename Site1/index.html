About Me

Welcome to Pin Photography, your number one source for all photographs. I am dedicated to providing you the very best of photographs, with an emphasis on fast and high quality.
Founded in 1984 by Hor Wai Pin, Pin Photography has come a long way from its beginnings in Malaysia. When Hor Wai Pin first started out, his passion for photography drove him to start his own business.

I hope you enjoy my photographs as much as I enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact me.
